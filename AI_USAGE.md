## ‚ú® Tips for Better Results

1. **Share Your Design System**
   - Paste color values
   - Share component examples
   - Explain naming conventions

2. **Be Progressive**
   - Start simple, then add complexity
   - Ask for one piece at a time
   - Build incrementally

3. **Ask for Explanations**
   - "Explain why you chose this approach"
   - "What are the benefits of this pattern?"
   - "How does this follow React best practices?"

4. **Request Variations**
   - "Can you create a dark mode version?"
   - "Show me an alternative implementation"
   - "What's another way to do this?"
